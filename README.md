## StageSuite (Featured Project)

**StageSuite** is a production management platform for theatre, designed to simplify scheduling, communication, and coordination across cast and crew.

It focuses on one of the hardest real-world problems:  
**building rehearsal schedules that actually work for everyone.**

Key ideas:
- Intelligent scheduling based on availability and constraints  
- Tools for directors, stage managers, and production teams  
- Designed around real theatre workflows, not generic project management  

Currently in active development as my final year project.
